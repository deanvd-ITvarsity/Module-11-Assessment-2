Rouladen

A traditional german dinner consisting of rolls made of thin beef slices rolled around bacon, onion, pickles, and mustard all roasted in red wine. It si best eaten with vegetables and mashed potatoes. This dish highlites the corner stones of german cuisine being a good endulgence in rich meat, while allowing for an introduction of starch.

City : Berlin

Rating : 4.1/5

Restaurant Name: The Huns Inn

Maultaschen

A german version on ravioli consisting of palm sized pastry filled with minced meat, bread crums, onions and spinach, all simmered and served with broth complimenting its interior of meat and ensuring moist pastry. These delicious pastries have become the regional specialty and has a wonderfull taste and texture.

City : Munich

Rating : 4/5

Restaurant Name: Germany's Barn Eatery

Schnitzel

A controversial and disputatious meal that is not short of die-hard fans. A juicy chicken breast coated in braed crums with optional spices, fried and served with fried potatoes and cold Franconian apple wine. This dishis both satisfactory and filling, never leaving one feeling in want of more. Sometimes a cream-based sauce is served alongside this dish to ensure their is no unnecessary dryness while consuming.

City : Berlin

Rating : 4.6/5

Restaurant Name: Heinz's Tavern
