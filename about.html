About FoodieFanatix

FoodieFanatix is an online cuisine review platform that combines a blog environment with a review site. It provides up-to-date and realistic reviews of various cherished dishes around the world! This platform also has a focus on photography as although we can't get viewers to taste the dishes; a visual representation will ensure viewers can identify the dish being reviewed!

FoodieFanatix chersihes its viewers/users and aims to build a strong and respectful community. Our site aims to reach people of different cultures and expose individuals to new cuisine and cultures or allow them to travel down a nostalgic road! We also encourage users to be vocal and active by reaching out to share opnions or educate both us and others about their cultural cuisines.

Our site is both user friendly and responsive ensuring users can access it from computers or mobile devices! Our focus is both food and people. User satisfaction is our top priority!

Our Rating System :

Our ratings are out 5, with 5 being the highest rating. Rating a dish considers 5 factors, with each factor contributing a point or less to its total rating. These factors include taste, texture, presentation, authenticity and The FoodieFanatix Seal of Approval. We are yet to give a dish 5/5 as neither culture produces superior cuisine and cooking is subject to human error. This rating system is used stringently and there is no room for bias compromises!
