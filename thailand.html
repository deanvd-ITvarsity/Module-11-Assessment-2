Bangkok, Thailand

This grand city that is the playground for thai cuisine and culture enthusiasts!

Tom Yum Goong (Spicy Shrimp Soup)

A bowl of steaming aromatic glory, created with lemograss, chilli,galangal, kaffier lime leaves, shallots, fresh lime juice and of course fifh sauce. All in the company of sea fresh prawns and musgrooms with coconut cream. This creates a dish symbolic of Thai cuisine and its richness.

City : Bangkok

Rating : 4.2/5

Restaurant Name: Oriental Express

Som Tam (Spicy Green Papaya Salad)

The most popular dish in Thailand and it is worthy of this great title. This vanguard consists of shredded green papaya, tomatoes, carrots, peanuts, dried shrimp, runner beans, palm sugar, tamarind pulp, fish sauce, lime juice, garlic and plenty of chillies. All these flavourful ingredients are mixed together in mortar pestle to draw out all the magnificant flavours.

City : Bangkok

Rating : 4.6/5

Restaurant Name: Bangkok National Eatery

Khao Pad (Fried Rice)

A u distinctive dish with minimal ingredients, but not minimal in its taste. This dish features a mixture of green onions, chillis/peppers, soy sauce,sambal, msg and of course rice fried togerther in a wok, in the optional presence of meat. The dish comes with cucumber and a variety of condaments.

City : Bangkok

Rating : 4.4/5

Restaurant Name: South East Wok
