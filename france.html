Boeuf Bourguignon

A wonderful red wine beef stew that is a family staple in France. The presence of a range of vegetables ensures there is break in texture and taste of pure beef stew. This dish is best served with Dauphinoise or Lyonnaise potatoes which compliment the stew and serve as starch support for the stew's moisture and richness.

City : Paris

Rating : 4/5

Restaurant Name: Paris Eatery

Coq Au Vin

A underated dish of chicken braised in red wine with bacon, butter, mushrooms and beef stock. The type of wine used depends on the region of France, this ensures variations of taste and uniqueness, and ensures that it distinguishes itself from the British chicken casserole. This dish is best served with creamed potatoes and buttered french beans.

City : Versailles

Rating : 4.4/5

Restaurant Name:King Louis Vineyard
