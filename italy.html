Ragu Alla Bolognese

The national dish of Italia and widely used staple. It consists of the special bolognese sauce made up of tomatoes, minced beef, garlic, wine, and herbs. This is all served with a selected pasta generally elongated and thin. A garnish is optional usually basil leaves. It is a wonderful dish with a noteworthy richness and distinctive taste.

City : Bologna

Rating : 4.3/5

Restaurant name: Antonio's Kitchen

Focaccia

A traditional italian bread made from flatened dough topped with cheese and tomatoes as well as basil, olives, and oregano garnishes. This bread is a wonderfull starter to your dining experience or as a partner with some rich red wine! This bread must NOT be underestimated it is full of flavour and will have you coming back for another slice!

City : Venice

Rating : 4/5

Restaurant Name: Ciao Bella

Lasagne

An extremely popular dish throughout the world, but italy knows how to make it right!
